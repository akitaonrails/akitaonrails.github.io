Folks, I just learned that 37signals published [our translation](http://gettingreal.37signals.com/GR_por.php) online for everyone to see. They made some changes; I thought they were going to wait until we finished.

Either way, the [post](http://www.37signals.com/svn/posts/244-update-on-getting-real-translations) on the Signals vs Noise blog shows the translation status from other countries. I'm proud to say we are WAY ahead — most others are still on the first chapter. We've already finished translating all 16 chapters and we're halfway through the revision.

That's why I called it "Release Candidate 2." RC1 was a restricted release, RC2 is now public, and in a few days we'll try to ship the final version!

Thanks to the translators: Herval Freire, Juraci Krohling Costa, Marcello Rocha, Diogo Bispo, Adriano Mitre, Ricardo Augusto, Rodrigo K.

And also the reviewers: Mateus Del Bianco, Diogo Bispo, Davis Zanetti Cabral, Gustavo Cardoso, Ricardo Augusto.
